Emerging Institutional Paradigms for the Digital Commons

數位資源共用的新興機構典範

Paul F. Uhlir
　
NRC Board on Research Data and Information
研究資料與資訊委員會
National Academy of Sciences
美國國家科學院

Paul F. Uhlir, J.D., is the director of NRC Board on Research Data and Information, at the National Academies in USA. He also serves on the IAP Program on Digital Knowledge Resources and Infrastructure in Developing Countries.


abstract / 摘要
  • English
  • 中文

It is axiomatic that the rate of change in technological systems initially outpaces human capacity to adapt to the technological advances. This is particularly true for transformational technologies that displace their antecedents, including the associated organizational paradigms. Such a
transformation is currently taking place as a result of the technological revolution brought about by the combination of digital information technologies and global communication networks. The pre-internet legacy business model for research journals and data centers that restricts access to and use of publicly funded scientific information, however, is not taking advantage of the potential benefits of national and global open availability online. Such a business model consequently is slowing the progress of science and socioeconomic development. The question, therefore, is not whether open availability to publicly-funded research information is better than access provided on terms that are economically, legally, and technologically restricted, but how open availability and re-use can be most effectively institutionalized and how quickly.

There are, in fact, multiple paths that are being taken to make this transition from restricted to open dissemination and use. The evolutionary human systems are thus beginning to respond to the opportunities made possible by the revolutionary technologies embodied in global digital networks. A gradual, but highly significant and far-reaching restructuring of scientific communication in public research is taking place. This presentation will review some of these institutional developments and their rationale, including some of the barriers and negative effects from not adopting the new digital commons paradigms.

[This presentation is based in part on one of my recent articles, “The emerging role of open repositories for scientific literature as a fundamental component of the public research infrastructure,” in Open Access: Open Problems, G. Sica (ed.), Polimetrica, Torino, Italy (2007). The views expressed here are those of the author and not necessarily those of the U.S. National Academy of Sciences.]

技術系統變遷的速率，一般公認，往往遠超過人們為因應技術進展，而能調整的程度。取代性的技術轉變，若涉及相關組織制度的典範轉移，這種情形尤其顯著。，這其中也。因為數位資訊技術與全球通訊網路的結合，所造成的技術革新，已帶來取代性的轉變。網際網路時代之前就存有的學術期刊與資料中心的事業模式，對公共資助所產出的科學資訊，採取限制取用的作法；對於全國性以及全球性的開放式資訊線上取用，所能帶來的潛在益處，並不重視。此種舊有的事業模式，事實上減緩了科學與社會經濟的發展。因此，問題的根本不在於公共資助所產出的科學資訊的開放取用方式，跟用以經濟、法律、與技術來限制取用的方式相比時，是否比較理想；而是開放取用方式，如何能快速的、有效的制度化。

事實上，目前已有多種途徑致力於將資訊的散布和使用，從限制式的方式移轉到開放式的方式。對於全球數位網路所帶來，眾多革命性技術的潛在可能，演化性的人類體制，也正逐步回應。一種漸進地、但卻具有高度意義與深遠影響的結構性改組，正發生在公共出資研究的科學傳播上。此次演講將回顧這部份的制度發展以及相關論述，並對於進入新的數位共用資源典範的障礙，以及不採行所產生的負面效應，做一評析。

[此次演講取材自本人最近的論文〈公共出資研究的基礎架構中，科學文獻開放典藏的新興角色〉，收錄於 G. Sica 所編輯，Polimetrica 出版的《開放近用：開放問題》一書 (Torino, Italy, 2007) 。本文表達的是本文作者之觀點，不必然是 U.S. National Academy of Sciences 的觀點。]

The Progress of an S&T Information Gateway for Taiwan

台灣科技資訊入口網站發展現況

Ted Lau / 劉聰德
　
Science and Technology Policy Research and Information Center (STPI)
科技政策研究與資訊中心
National Applied Research Laboratory (NARL)
國家實驗研究院

Dr. Ted Lau earned his Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio. He had more than 20 years of working experience in computer, telephony and satellite communication network systems in the United States before returning to Taiwan to join the STPI in 2005. Currently, Dr. Lau serves as the lead project manager for the S&T Information Gateway for Taiwan Project, funded by NARL. He also is the lead project manager for a joint project between Taiwan and Russia aimed at conducting comparative analyses on their respective national R&D and innovation systems and technological development priorities.


abstract / 摘要
  • English
  • 中文

The Science and Technology Policy Research and Information (STPI) Center of Taiwan’s National Applied Research Laboratory (NARL) is working towards launching an open access science and technology (S&T) information website. This effort would serve as the S&T information gateway to Taiwan among world scientific communities.

Item 20 in the Science and Technology Basic Law of Taiwan, which became effective in January 1999, stated that “the government should set up policy for sharing S&T information and take an integral approach for creating an information network system encompassing domestic as well as foreign S&T research and development efforts.” While progress has been gradual, implementation of this particular piece of legislation continues.

Before the law was passed, several databases of research papers, technical reports, and numeric data had already been maintained in various research organizations and were publicly accessible. In 2004, TWAREN (TaiWan Advanced Research and Education Network), a high quality network specifically designed to serve the research and education communities, was deployed by the National Center for High-performance Computing under NARL to be the communication infrastructure for the eventual “integral system” that was mandated by the law. The current activities represent a first step towards building a website to integrate Taiwan’s S&T information and research results within NARL and to allow open access to its database by the public. This presentation will provide an overview of these activities and future directions.

國家實驗研究院（國研院）科技政策研究與資訊中心正在進行建構一個合乎開放近用 (open access) 的科技資訊網站，並期待它能發展成為順應潮流的台灣科技資訊入口網站，以服務全球的科學界。

台灣政府在1999年元月公布實施的《科學技術基本法》，其中第20條規定：「為推動科學技術研究發展，政府應擬訂科學技術資訊流通政策，採取整體性計畫措施，建立國內外科學技術研究發展之相關資訊網路及資訊體系，並應培育資訊相關處理人才，以利科學技術資訊之充實及有效利用。」雖然進度不快，但台灣政府已持續推動科技資訊的流通和公開使用。

在政府公布科技基本法之前，有幾個機構已經收集一些研究論文、技術報告和數據資料供公開使用。2004年國科會責成國研院下的國家高速網路與計算中心規劃建置一學術研究專用的「台灣高品質學術研究網路(TWAREN)」，此網路正可做為未來的資訊流通「整合系統」的基礎通訊架構。目前的努力著重於建設一個涵蓋全國研院科技資訊和研究成果，並合乎開放近用的科技資訊網站。本演講將報告計畫執行的進展現況及未來的發展方向。
