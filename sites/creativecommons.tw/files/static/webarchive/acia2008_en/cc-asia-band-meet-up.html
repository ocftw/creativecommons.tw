Where, When, Whom

Location: Lounge & Cafe
Address: Academic Activities Center, Academia Sinica, 128 Academia Road, Section 2, Nangang 115, Taipei

Time: January 19, 2007, 2:00 - 4:00 PM
Contact: Pig Head Skin (pigheadskin.tw @ yahoo . com . tw)

What

Artists, musicians, producers, techies, etc. will meet during the ACIA workshop to discuss the idea and realization of a “CC Asia Band”. The idea of a “CC Asia Band” has been, for some time, with the many people who are porting the Creative Commons Licenses to jurisdictions in Asia.

It is hoped that the band will be a showcase of cultural diversity in Asia, and its works will help bring the voices from this region to the globe. It is planned that an album will be produced by the band and released under a Creative Commons License. This album shall be distributed via multiple channels so as to reach its audience widely.
307
Musicians in the to-be-formed band will be multi-talented artists: Each will write original songs (melody and lyrics), as well as play and record the songs with other band members in a truly collaborative style. The band will consist of top electric guitar players, bass players, keyboard players, drummers, and vocalists.
