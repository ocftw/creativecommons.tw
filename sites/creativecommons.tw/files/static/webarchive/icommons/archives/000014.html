1.Collective Work翻成集合著作或編輯著作,何者為宜?

cc license中 collective work 如何翻譯？


"Collective Work" means a work, such as a periodical issue, anthology or encyclopedia, in which the Work in its entirety in unmodified form, along with a number of other contributions, constituting separate and independent works in themselves, are assembled into a collective whole. A work that constitutes a Collective Work will not be considered a Derivative Work (as defined below) for the purposes of this License.

Collective Work在美國著作權法亦有規定:
U.S. Copyright Law:
A “collective work” is a work, such as a periodical issue, anthology, or encyclopedia, in which a number of contributions, constituting separate and independent works in themselves, are assembled into a collective whole.
A “compilation” is a work formed by the collection and assembling of preexisting materials or of data that are selected, coordinated, or arranged in such a way that the resulting work as a whole constitutes an original work of authorship.
The term “compilation” includes collective works.
美國著作權法在collective work之外,另外針對compilation作定義,並規定compilation包括collective work.因此台灣有人將collective work翻成"集合著作",以有別於"編輯"的概念。

但是台灣的著作權法並沒有"集合著作"的用語,只有"編輯著作"的概念:
著作權法第七條:「就資料之選擇及編排具有創作性者為編輯著作，以獨立之著作保護之。編輯著作之保護，對其所收編著作之著作權不生影響。」

因此就必須探討美國著作權法和iCommons所規定的Collective Work是否等同於台灣著作權法中所規定的編輯著作?

Collective Work在美國著作權法和iCommons中,細部規定雖有所不同,但是和台灣的編輯著作相較,同樣都是指資料的選擇及編排;另外美國著作權法在對於"編輯"的定義中指出,編輯之後的產出成果整體上亦必須具備原創性,而既然Collective Work包括在編輯的概念之中,從而Collective Work就資料的選擇與編排亦必須符合具備原創性的要件。這樣的解釋結果和台灣"編輯著作"的概念並沒有不同,因此初步決定採用"編輯著作"的翻譯用語,而不翻"集合著作",以免在和國內法作銜接時,產生解釋上的困難。
