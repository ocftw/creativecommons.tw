尊重原作者-非商業利用-相同方式分享 1.0 中譯暨說明

1. "Edited Work" means a work, such as a periodical issue, anthology or encyclopedia, in which the Work in its entirety in unmodified form, along with a number of other contributions, constituting separate and independent works in themselves, are assembled into a collective whole. A work that constitutes an Edited Work will not be considered a Derivative Work (as defined below) for the purposes of this License.

1."編輯著作"意指像期刊、選集或百科全書的著作，其整體以未經修改的形式，與其中的數個作品，組成各自分開而且獨立的著作。這些作品被彙編成為一個集合的整體。因本授權條款之目的，構成編輯著作的作品不會被認為是一個衍生著作(定義如下)。

Collective Work ="Edited work"
The definition and examples defined in “Collective Work” are found in the definition and examples of “Edited Work” in ROC Copyright Law, i.e. independent contributions assembled into a collective whole, and the choosing and editing of the materials are of originality.

陳舜伶（shunling），張容綺（joychang）與黃靖元(JAENSCH)完成了 Creative Commons 授權條款「重原作者-非商業利用-相同方式分享 1.0 (Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 1.0) 」中譯與英譯對照的說明（pdf 檔）。我們預計將於本週內開始透過 mailing list 進行公開的線上討論。
