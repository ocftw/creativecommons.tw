大會議程 » 完全開放式行動電話的誕生

內容概要

於目前開放原始碼的氛圍下，如何經由「我本墨客」的發展而達成全然開放的行動電話？此次簡報將說明我們的雄心壯志如何達成、妥協為何產生、以及經驗怎樣學得的歷程。

（莊庭瑞譯）

講者介紹

照片來自Imran Ali ( CC:BY-NC-ND 2.0)

Sean Moss-Pultz

President
OpenMoko, Inc.

Sean Moss-Pultz is a surfer boy that ended up washed ashore in Taiwan. He's had a blast living there for more than four years now. Currently, Sean is the leader of the Mobility Division inside of First International Computer and President of OpenMoko, Inc. in Taiwan.

OpenMoko make fun (open source-based) products that people can personalize to their hearts content. Their first device is Neo — the world’s first totally open mobile phone.
